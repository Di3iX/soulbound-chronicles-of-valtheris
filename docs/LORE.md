# Lore

# The World of Valtheris

Valtheris is an ancient world where magic, forgotten kingdoms and dark creatures exist together.

Long ago the world was protected by the Soulbound — legendary warriors who were connected to powerful souls.

A great catastrophe shattered the balance of the world.

Ancient kingdoms fell.

Dark creatures spread across the land.

Many cities became ruins.

Now new heroes must restore the balance.
